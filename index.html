About Me

I am a physical chemist with interests in solvation science, spectroscopy, and energy research. My work bridges fundamental chemistry and real-world applications, especially in biophysical processes and electrochemical systems.

Research Proposal

Solvation Science in Biophysical and Electrochemical Systems

I aim to explore the role of solvents in phase separation of proteins and the optimization of electrolyte solvation in Li-ion batteries. The research involves advanced spectroscopy, such as THz, and X-ray scattering.
